Automated Tank War Client

The Tanks is a automated tank game. A server will listen to five or less number of tank clients as they battle it out in a 20 x 20 grid. Each tank will have their own AI to compete and a GUI for the benefit of the observers. knaTank is our tank client.

Artificial Intelligence

The tank is pitted against three other tanks and the winner after they battle it out will advance to the next round.

Network Communication

The tank will communicate with the server to get the game updates and to make its move.

Graphics

The tank game is given a Graphical User Interface for the peers to observe the game in real time.

Game Rules


One move per second

Each tank is allowed to make one move per second and after each second, the server sends the game updates to all the tank clients

Bricks, Stonesand Water

Stones are invincible, bricks are shattered after 4 hits and water is uncrossable. Points will be deducted if stones and bricks are hit and the tank will die if it tries to cross water.

Life Packs and Coins

Life packs will add one tenth of the initial health to the tank. Coins will add points and money to the tank. If a tank dies, half its points will be available for plunder.

Turns

A tank could turn 90 degrees, 180 degrees or 270 degrees, move forward or shoot one bullet per turn. Server will only accept the first move sent of each second.

Shooting

Bullets shot will travel 3 squares per second and each bullet will decrease the health of a tank by 0.1 of the initial health if hit.

Winning

The tank with the most number of points after 15 minutes of play will win. The points will be adjusted to compensate for each computer's processing power and memory.

Tanks come in two forms: the dangerous, deadly kind and the 'liberating' kind.
